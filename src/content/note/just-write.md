**Can I publish 100 posts on my personal blog in a year?** I've decided to find out by taking part in [#100DaysToOffload](https://100daystooffload.com/).

As a recovering perfectionist, I'm setting the bar intentionally low: Any post or note published on my personal website within the next 365 days will count towards my goal.

> Posts don’t need to be long-form, deep, meaningful, or even that well written. If there are spelling and grammar mistakes, or even if there’s no real point to the post, so what? What’s important is that you’re writing about the things you want to write about.

Once I've published a post, I will share a link on [my Mastodon account](https://social.tchncs.de/@sabineemden).
