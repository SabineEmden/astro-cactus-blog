**This is my home on the World Wide Web.**

On my personal website, I write about web development:

- to deepen my understanding,
- to share my knowledge and insights,
- to build a knowledge repository, and
- to document my progress,
- to improve my writing skills.

Topics include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive web design, accessibility, career management, job search, productivity, and anything else that catches my attention while I learn and work on projects.

I write for others who are learning web development, for my future self to be able to go back through my notes, for my present self to develop a better understanding of the topics I write about, and to develop a writing habit.

I hope my writing improves the more posts I publish, just like my coding improves the more projects I build.
